# Assignment 2 — Research-Driven Email Sequence

## Client
ANSR — global GCC (Global Capability Center) enablement and consulting firm.

## Background
Attached is a set of research notes on the GCC expansion market — buyer pain points, objections, ICP signals, and what messaging tends to resonate with prospective clients. This mirrors the kind of raw research input ANSR's growth/marketing team would work from.

## Dataset
[`research/gcc_market_research_example.md`](research/gcc_market_research_example.md)

## Your Task
Using the research notes as your foundation, draft a **multi-email outbound sequence** targeted at ANSR's ICP (CXOs at global enterprises evaluating whether to build a GCC in India).

Your sequence should:
1. Define the target persona(s) and the trigger/context for outreach (based on the ICP signals and buying committee notes).
2. Map a logical sequence (minimum 4-5 emails) with clear intent per email — e.g., problem-awareness, proof/case study, objection handling, urgency/CTA.
3. Write full copy for each email — subject line + body — grounded in the specific pain points and objections in the research notes (not generic outbound copy).
4. Specify cadence/timing between emails and any branching logic (e.g., what changes if the prospect opens but doesn't reply vs. doesn't open at all).
5. Note how you'd personalize each email per persona (CFO vs. CTO vs. CHRO) if targeting more than one buyer role.

## Deliverables
1. Persona/ICP summary (brief)
2. Full email sequence with copy, subject lines, and cadence
3. Short rationale note: which research findings drove which messaging choices
